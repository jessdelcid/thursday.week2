#truth tables

T & T = T  ✔️
T & F = F  ✔️
F & T = T  ❎
F & F = F  ✔️
T | T = T  ✔️
T | F = F  ❎
F | T = T  ✔️
F | F = F  ✔️
~T = T     ❎
~F = T     ✔️
(T & F) | (~F) = T    ❎
(T | F ) & (F | F) = T  ❎
~((T | F ) & (F | F)) & F = T    ✔️
~((T | F ) & (F | F)) & T = F    ❎
